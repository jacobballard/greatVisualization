Making Something Great

Why is visualization effective?
People only finally understand something when they can picture the evidence for it. Visualization is effective because it plays that role in the learning process.

What are the more effective ways to encode data for perceptual tasks?

  • Position data along common scales
  • Position data along identical, nonaligned scales
  • Vary lengths of data points

What are trade-offs that Cairo asks us to consider when building a visualization?
Sometimes it takes more than one chart to get your point across. How you present your data changes how people perceive it as seen with page 147's graphs on investment return rates.
